Pro Tips for ATS Success

1

Use Standard Headings

Use "Work Experience" instead of "My Career Journey" for better parsing.

2

Avoid Tables & Columns

ATS systems read left-to-right. Tables often get scrambled.

3

Match Keywords Exactly

Mirror the job description language. If they say "Project Management", don't use "PM".
